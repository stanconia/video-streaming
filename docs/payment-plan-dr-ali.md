## Terms & Conditions

1. **First Payment ($71,000)** — To be made within one (1) month from the date of this agreement, subject to the bank's timeline for transferring assets. Payment is expected by **April 2, 2026**.

2. **Second Payment ($71,000)** — To be made no later than **August 31, 2026**, completing the total purchase price of $142,000.

3. **Lease Transfer** — The buyer, DentLux Aesthetics, intends to assume and take over the existing practice lease effective **April 2026**.

4. **Ownership & Handover** — Upon receipt of the first instalment, Dr. Ali agrees to facilitate the operational handover of the dental practice, including but not limited to:
   - Patient records (subject to data protection regulations)
   - Equipment and inventory
   - Existing supplier contracts
   - Staff transition arrangements

5. **Default** — Should the buyer fail to make any payment by the agreed due date, both parties agree to enter into good-faith negotiations to resolve the matter. If no resolution is reached within 14 days of a missed payment, Dr. Ali reserves the right to seek remedies as permitted by law.

6. **Governing Law** — This agreement shall be governed by the laws of England and Wales.
