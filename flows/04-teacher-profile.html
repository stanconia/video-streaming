About

Hello! I'm Sarah, a passionate mathematics educator with over 8 years of experience teaching students from 3rd to 8th grade. I believe that every child can excel at math when given the right tools and encouragement.

My teaching philosophy centers on making math fun and relatable through real-world examples, interactive games, and patient guidance. I specialize in helping students who struggle with math anxiety overcome their fears and discover the joy of problem-solving.
